Delete Your PikTag Account

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Overview

PikTag Inc. respects your right to delete your account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and what happens to your data.

Option 1: Delete In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way is directly inside the PikTag mobile app:

  1. Open PikTag on your device and sign in
  2. Tap the Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us from the address associated with your PikTag account:

Email privacy@pikt.ag

Please include:

  • The email address or phone number you used to sign up
  • Your PikTag username (if known)
  • A brief statement confirming you want your account deleted

We will respond within 7 business days and complete deletion within 30 days of verification.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Account information (email, phone, name, username, profile photo)
  • Profile data (bio, social links, biolinks)
  • All tags you created and tag activity history
  • QR scan history and connection data
  • Uploaded photos and user-generated content
  • Synced contacts and location data
  • Device push notification tokens
  • In-app messages and interactions

What Data Is Retained (and Why)

Certain data may be retained for a limited period after deletion for legal, security, or operational reasons:

  • Anonymized analytics — aggregate usage data that cannot identify you (retained indefinitely for product improvement)
  • Crash & diagnostic logs — retained up to 90 days to investigate app stability issues
  • Fraud & abuse records — retained as required to prevent account re-creation by banned users
  • Legal compliance records — retained only if and as long as required by applicable law

Deletion Timeline

  • Account becomes inaccessible: immediately
  • User-facing data removed from PikTag servers: within 30 days
  • Backups purged: within 90 days

Before You Delete

Deletion is permanent. Once complete, you cannot recover your account, username, connections, or any tags you created. If you just need a break, you can sign out or uninstall the app without deleting your account.
