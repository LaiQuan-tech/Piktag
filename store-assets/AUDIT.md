# Play Store Asset Audit

## Generated

- **icon-512.png** (source: `mobile/assets/icon.png`, 512x512, 49 KB, 32-bit PNG with alpha)
  - Source was 1024x1024 — downscaled with Lanczos (ImageMagick default). No quality loss.
- **feature-graphic-1024x500.png** (composed from `mobile/assets/logo-icon.png` + gradient + text, 1024x500, 161 KB, PNG)
  - Background: diagonal gradient `#aa00ff` (top-left) to `#8c52ff` (bottom-right), 135deg flow.
  - Logo: `mobile/assets/logo-icon.png` recolored to solid white (preserving alpha), 240px tall, positioned at (90, 130).
  - Text (PingFang TC, white): "PikTag" at 130pt, tagline "用標籤記住每個人" at 42pt in `#f0e8ff`.

## Missing — require user action

  - No current-brand phone screenshots exist in the repo.

They meet the Play Store technical minimum (320px short side) and aspect ratio constraints, so they could be used as a fallback, but ship quality will suffer.

## Notes / caveats

- `mobile/assets/icon.png`, `adaptive-icon.png`, `splash-icon.png` are all byte-identical (md5-equal 160255-byte files) — same source image reused for all slots. That source is the finished purple gradient icon.
- Two duplicate asset trees exist: `mobile/assets/` (current, correct purple gradient) and root-level `assets/` (older, different icon). Used `mobile/assets/` as it matches the app branding.
- Feature graphic uses PingFang TC from the system (`/System/Library/.../PingFang.ttc`) — default weight was used because the `.ttc` collection face-index selection is unreliable via ImageMagick. Typography reads as semibold-ish due to the large point size. If you want a heavier weight, substitute a manually-specified Source Han Sans or Noto Sans TC Bold.
- Logo in feature graphic was flattened to pure white to contrast against the purple background; original logo has a pink-to-purple gradient that would have blended into the background.
- `mobile/ios/PikTag/Images.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/App-Icon-1024x1024@1x.png` exists (1024x1024, generated by Expo) but was not used as the icon source — it matches `mobile/assets/icon.png`.
- No web renders in `dist/_expo/` matched Play Store needs.
