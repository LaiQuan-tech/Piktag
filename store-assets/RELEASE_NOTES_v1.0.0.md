## Full release notes (long-form, for landing-page changelog / blog)

### en

**PikTag 1.0 — Pick your people.**

PikTag is a tag-first social CRM. Instead of asking "do I have their phone
number?", PikTag answers "who do I know that does X?". It's the network
layer between contacts and discovery — the part of your social graph that
LinkedIn ignores and your phone book can't see.

**What you can do in 1.0**

**Define yourself with tags.** Pick up to 10 tags that capture who you are
— `#photographer`, `#climbs-V5`, `#cofounder` — and pin the one that matters
most. PikTag's AI suggests tags from your bio, so getting started takes
seconds.

**Post an Ask.** Got a need? Tap the `+` on your avatar, type one
sentence ("Looking for a senior backend engineer who's done payments"),
and PikTag fans it out to the friends in your network whose tags match.
Asks expire in 24 hours. The right person typically surfaces in less.

**Reverse search.** The search bar's placeholder rotates through prompts
like "Need a friend who shoots photos" and "Designers near me", because
the way to find people on PikTag isn't by name — it's by what you need.
Tap a tag, see everyone in your network with that tag.

**Smart CRM.** Birthday reminders the day-of (sent at 9am local). Date-tag
anniversaries that surface "you met X one year ago today" automatically.
Mutual tags between you and a friend get highlighted on their profile so
the social signal — "we share this" — never disappears into the noise.

**QR exchange.** Open PikTag, show your code, the other person is
connected. All your biolinks (Instagram, LinkedIn, phone, web, plus any
custom platform) come along automatically. No typing, no business cards,
no "what was their last name again".

**Hidden tags.** Private annotations on each connection that only you can
see — `#met-at-Q3-mixer`, `#great-bbq-rec`, `#friend-of-Sarah`. PikTag
groups them by date, location, and frequently-used so your hands stay on
the rhythm of remembering, not on a keyboard.

**15 languages.** Traditional & Simplified Chinese, English, Japanese,
Korean, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Bengali, Hindi,
Indonesian, Thai, Turkish. The whole UI is localized — including the
rotating search prompts, which use the user's current location to
substitute "near you" with their actual city.

**Coming soon:** shareable bio profiles at `pikt.ag/:username`,
auto-import from LinkedIn / Instagram, deeper tag-based recommendations.

We read every piece of feedback. Tell us what's missing, what's broken,
and what would make you tell a friend about PikTag.

— Pick. Tag. Connect.

### zh-TW

**PikTag 1.0：找對的人，從標籤開始。**

PikTag 是一款用標籤連結人脈的社交 CRM。通訊錄解決「我有他電話嗎？」；PikTag
解決「我認識誰會 X？」。它是介於通訊錄與探索之間的那一層——LinkedIn 看不到、
電話本搜不出來的那一層。

**1.0 你可以做什麼**

**用標籤定義自己。** 選最多 10 個標籤代表你——`#攝影師`、`#V5 攀岩`、
`#共同創辦人`——並把最核心的一個釘上去。PikTag 的 AI 會從你的 bio
推薦標籤，幾秒內就能完成。

**發 Ask。** 有需求？點大頭照的 `+`，打一句話（「找做過支付的資深後端」），
PikTag 自動 fan-out 給標籤匹配的朋友。Ask 24 小時自動過期，但通常更短時間
內對的人就會自然冒出來。

**反向搜尋。** 搜尋框的 placeholder 會輪播「我需要懂攝影的朋友」、「在台北
附近的設計師」這類提示——因為 PikTag 找人的方式不是靠名字，是靠**你需要什麼**。
點一個標籤，看到你網絡裡所有有這個標籤的人。

**智慧 CRM。** 生日當天早上 9 點自動提醒。日期標籤週年（「一年前的今天你
認識了 X」）主動現身。共通標籤在朋友頁面紫色高亮——「我們共有這個」這個
社交訊號，永遠不會被淹沒。

**QR 互掃。** 打開 PikTag、亮出 QR、對方掃一下就連上。你的所有 biolinks
（IG、LinkedIn、電話、網站、自訂平台）一起跟過去。免打字、免名片、再也
不會「咦他姓什麼」。

**隱藏標籤。** 為每個朋友寫的私人筆記只有你看得到——`#在 Q3 mixer 認識`、
`#推薦的好吃排骨`、`#Sarah 的朋友`。PikTag 把這些自動分成日期、地點、常用
三類，讓你的手停在記憶的節奏上，不在鍵盤上。

**15 種語言。** 繁中、簡中、英、日、韓、西、法、葡、俄、阿、孟、印、印尼、
泰、土。整個 UI 都翻譯過——包括輪播搜尋提示，會用你目前的位置自動把「我附近」
代換成你所在的城市。

**即將推出：** 可分享的個人連結頁 `pikt.ag/:username`、從 LinkedIn / IG 自
動匯入、更深的標籤推薦演算法。

每一則回饋我們都會看。告訴我們還缺什麼、哪裡怪怪的、什麼功能會讓你想推薦
給朋友。

— Pick. Tag. Connect.

## What changed since the original 2026-04-17 draft

The first draft of this file was written before:

- The brand voice consolidated to "Pick. Tag. Connect." / "Pick your
  people." / "Search by need, not by name." (was: "用標籤記住每個人")
- Ask broadcasts shipped (notify_ask_posted DB trigger + AskStoryRow
  cards on the Connections tab)
- Reverse-lookup search shipped (rotating placeholder + GPS-derived
  city substitution)
- Welcome-slides onboarding shipped (3-mission concept teaching before
  the bio/tags data-collection flow)
- Mutual-tag highlighting shipped on FriendDetail
- 7-day expiry on the "new" badge shipped
- Date-tag-anniversary cron + birthday-check cron stabilized
- The 3/7/30 day follow-up reminders were REMOVED from the product
  (over-aggressive, inconsistent with the "lightweight CRM" stance)
- Contract-expiry reminder was REMOVED (out of scope for v1)
- Splash-screen hand-off rebuilt v3 (no logo jump or color-blink at
  native → JS transition)

The release-notes copy above reflects the actual shipped product as of
the v1.0.0 cut.
