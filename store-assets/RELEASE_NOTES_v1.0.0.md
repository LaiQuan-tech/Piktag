## Full release notes

### English (for internal / future App Store)

**PikTag 1.0 — say hi, stay in touch.**

PikTag is a new way to meet people and actually remember them. Instead of swapping phone numbers you'll never call, you swap a QR code — and everything you need to stay in touch lives in one place.

**What you can do in 1.0**

- **Instant QR exchange.** Open PikTag, show your code, and the other person is saved to your network in a tap. No typing, no typos, no "what was their last name again?"
- **#Hashtag organization.** Tag people the way you actually think about them — `#climbing`, `#coffee-shop-regulars`, `#wedding-guests`. Filter your network by any tag in seconds.
- **Friend map.** Opt in to share your general area with friends who also opt in. See who's nearby when you're traveling or free for lunch. Sharing is off by default and you control who sees what.
- **Reminders that actually matter.** Birthdays, anniversaries, contract renewals — set them once and PikTag nudges you before the date slips by.
- **Four privacy levels per link.** Every contact exchange has a visibility setting, so your work contacts don't have to see your personal life and vice versa.
- **Dark mode.** Looks great at midnight, looks great at noon.
- **15 languages.** English, 繁體中文, 简体中文, 日本語, 한국어, हिन्दी, العربية, বাংলা, Português, Español, Français, Bahasa Indonesia, Русский, ไทย, Türkçe.

**Coming soon:** shareable bio link profiles at `pikt.ag/:username`.

Thanks for trying the very first version. We read every piece of feedback — tell us what's missing, what's broken, and what you'd pay money for.

### 繁體中文（正體中文，台灣用語）

**PikTag 1.0 正式上線——輕鬆打招呼，長久保持聯繫。**

PikTag 是一個新的認識朋友、記住朋友的方式。與其交換一輩子不會打的電話號碼，不如互掃 QR Code——從此所有保持聯繫需要的資訊，都集中在一個地方。

**1.0 你可以做什麼**

- **QR Code 快速交換。** 打開 PikTag、亮出你的 QR Code，對方掃一下就存進彼此的人脈網。免打字、免拼錯、再也不會忘記對方姓什麼。
- **#Hashtag 分類人脈。** 用你自己的方式標記朋友，例如 `#攀岩`、`#常去咖啡店`、`#婚禮賓客`。幾秒內就能篩出任何一群人。
- **好友地圖。** 選擇性開啟後，可以和同樣開啟的朋友分享大致位置。出差或週末有空時，一眼看見誰就在附近。預設完全關閉，你決定誰能看見你。
- **真正有用的提醒。** 生日、紀念日、合約到期日——設定一次，PikTag 會在時間到之前輕輕提醒你。
- **每筆聯絡資訊 4 種隱私權限。** 每一次交換都可以設定可見度，工作上的聯絡人不必看到你的私生活，反之亦然。
- **深色模式。** 半夜舒服、白天也舒服。
- **15 種語言。** English、繁體中文、简体中文、日本語、한국어、हिन्दी、العربية、বাংলা、Português、Español、Français、Bahasa Indonesia、Русский、ไทย、Türkçe。

**即將推出：** 可分享的個人連結頁 `pikt.ag/:username`。

感謝你試用這最初的版本。你的每一則回饋我們都會看——告訴我們還缺什麼、哪裡怪怪的、你願意付錢換什麼功能。
